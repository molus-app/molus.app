## We're here to save the world
We live in a world marked by selfishness. Everywhere we look, there is self-obsessed behavior and its inevitable outcomes: climate change, poverty, and general apathy. The cause is a misunderstanding of human nature, and Molus exists to nurture the transformation: continual self realization.

## Our lives are not our own
Humans—like a sprouting tree—have a fundamental nature we are born to realize. Over our lifetimes, we change: growing, branching, and eventually, falling. Every season changes our leaves. Yet they all promoted new growth—even if it was not our own. Our recognition of this fact, is what it means to be a fully realized human being.
