## We are here to save the world
The world is marked by selfishness. Everywhere we look, there is self-obsession and its inevitable outcomes: war, poverty, and general apathy. The cause is a misunderstanding of human nature, and Molus exists to nurture the transformation.

## Continual self realization
Humans—like a sprouting tree—have a fundamental nature we are born to realize. Over lifetimes, we change: growing, branching, and eventually, falling. Every season changes our lives. Yet all promote growth—even if it is not ours. Accepting this fact, is what it means to be fully realized.

## Show us your true self
Molus promotes art to realize the [great way](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indra%27s_net). We work with artists that help others come to self realization. Please contact us at [contact@molus.app](mailto:contact@molus.app) if you think we can be of service.
