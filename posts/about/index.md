## The world needs us
Everywhere we look, there is self-obsession and its inevitable outcomes: war, poverty, and general apathy. The cause is a misunderstanding of human nature, and Molus exists to nurture the transformation.

## Continual realization
Humans—like a sprouting tree—have a fundamental nature we are born to realize. Over lifetimes, we change: growing, branching, and eventually, falling. Every season changes our lives. Yet all promote growth—even if it is not our own. Accepting this, is what it means to continually realize.

## Show your true self
