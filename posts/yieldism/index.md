---
title: "Yieldism"
date: 2026-06-12
---

Yielding is the source of truth and deception. Love and hate.

To yield is what it means to be human.

When we yield, our body becomes an instrument of a greater force, which may be used for good or evil. 

Discerning is the answer.

Beyond good and evil, the way of Christ, Bhudda, and all enlightened beings awaits us.

Don't overthink it. Choose to discern, for our humanity depends on it.

Who is worthy of service, if not yourself and the world: at once.

Who is God?
